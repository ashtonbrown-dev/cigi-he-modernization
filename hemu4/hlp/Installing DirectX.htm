Installing DirectX

The CIGI Host Emulator uses the DirectInput component of DirectX to enable joystick support. For instructions on downloading and installing DirectX 8.0 or higher, go to Microsoft's DirectX home page at http://www.microsoft.com/directx/.

Note: DirectX is required by both the Real-Time and Windows versions of the CIGI Host Emulator.
