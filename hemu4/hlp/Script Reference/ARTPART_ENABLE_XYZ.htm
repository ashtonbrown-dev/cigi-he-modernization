ARTPART_ENABLE_XYZ

Enables or disables translation of articulated part along its X, Y, and Z axes.

Syntax

ARTPART_ENABLE_XYZ    entity_id    part_id    x_enable    y_enable    z_enable

Parameters

entity_id

An integer specifying the entity to which the articulated part belongs.

part_id

An integer specifying the articulated part.

x_enable

An integer specifying whether the X offset may be changed. May be one of the following values:

    0 — Disabled

    1 — Enabled

y_enable

An integer specifying whether the Y offset may be changed. May be one of the following values:

    0 — Disabled

    1 — Enabled

z_enable

An integer specifying whether the Z offset may be changed. May be one of the following values:

    0 — Disabled

    1 — Enabled
