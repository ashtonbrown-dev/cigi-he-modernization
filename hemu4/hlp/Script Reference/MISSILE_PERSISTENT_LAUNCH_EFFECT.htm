MISSILE_PERSISTENT_LAUNCH_EFFECT

This command adds the specified animation entity to the missile's persistent launch effect list. The animation will be played when the missile is launched and will continue to play out its animation sequence after the missile reaches its target.

Syntax

MISSILE_PERSISTENT_LAUNCH_EFFECT    missile_id    animation_id

Parameters

missile_id

An integer that specifies the entity ID of the missile.

animation_id

An integer that specifies the entity ID of the animation.

Remarks

This command differs from MISSILE_TERMINATING_LAUNCH_EFFECT in that terminating launch effects are stopped and hidden after the missile hits its target.

Any given missile may have more than one persistent launch effect.
