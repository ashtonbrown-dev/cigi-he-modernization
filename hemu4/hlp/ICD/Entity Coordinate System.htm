Entity Coordinate System

Each entity has a local NED coordinate system as shown below in Figure 19. The origin corresponds to the entity’s reference point, typically the center of gravity. The +X axis extends out the "front" of the entity (e.g., the nose of an aircraft). The +Y axis extends out the right side, and the +Z axis extends out the bottom of the entity.

Entity Local Coordinate System

An entity’s local coordinate system is sometimes called its "body" coordinate system.
