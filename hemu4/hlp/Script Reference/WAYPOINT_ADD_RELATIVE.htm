WAYPOINT_ADD_RELATIVE

Defines a waypoint as a vector from an entity. The waypoint may be added to the waypoint list of one or more entities with the ENTITY_ADD_WAYPOINT command.

Syntax

WAYPOINT_ADD_RELATIVE    waypoint_id    bearing    decl    range

Parameters

waypoint_id

An integer specifying the ID to be assigned to the waypoint.

bearing

The bearing to the new waypoint. This is specified as an angle in degrees from the entity's positive X axis along its XY plane such that a bearing of +90° corresponds to a point along the positive Y axis.

decl

The declination to the new waypoint. This is specified as an angle in degrees from the entity's XY plane such that a bearing of +90° corresponds to a point along the negative Z axis (i.e., above the entity).

range

The length in meters of the vector from the entity to the waypoint.

Remarks:

When an entity reads a relative waypoint from its waypoint list, the geodetic position of the waypoint is calculated from the entity's current position.
