Resetting the CIGI Host Emulator and Image Generator

To reset both the CIGI Host Emulator and the Image Generator, click the Reset All button on the main toolbar. All entities in the scenario are destroyed and the current scenario is reloaded from disk. The terrain and entity geometry on the IG are deleted and reloaded. All environmental parameters, view properties, and component and articulated part states are reset to their default values. All rates previously applied to entities and articulated parts are lost. When the CIGI Host Emulator is placed back into Run mode, all environmental parameters, view properties, and entity states saved in the scenario file are sent to the IG.
