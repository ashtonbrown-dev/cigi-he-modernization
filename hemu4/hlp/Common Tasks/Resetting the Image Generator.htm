CIGI Host Emulator 2.1

Resetting the Image Generator

To reset the Image Generator, click the Reset IG button on the main toolbar. This removes all terrain and entity geometry and reloads it from disk. All views are reset to their default configurations. All environmental parameters such as time of day are reset to their default values.  All component (entity, database, and system) and articulated part states are reset to their modeled defaults. All rates previously applied to entities and articulated parts are lost.
