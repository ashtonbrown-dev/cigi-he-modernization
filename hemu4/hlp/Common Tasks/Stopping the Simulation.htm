Stopping the Simulation

To pause or end the simulation, click the Freeze Exercise button on the main toolbar. All entities, including special effects and weather, are suspended. Any packets explicitly added through the Test window are placed in a queue until the simulation is resumed. Note that the time of day and any entities or articulated parts to which a rate has been applied are not frozen on the IG.

Press F6 to freeze the exercise. The keyboard's media Play/Pause key toggles between freezing and running while HEMU has keyboard focus. Press F5 to run or resume explicitly.
