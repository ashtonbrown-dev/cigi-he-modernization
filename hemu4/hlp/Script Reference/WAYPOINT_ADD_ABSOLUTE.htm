WAYPOINT_ADD_ABSOLUTE

Defines a waypoint as a geodetic position. The waypoint may be added to the waypoint list of one or more entities with the ENTITY_ADD_WAYPOINT command.

Syntax

WAYPOINT_ADD_ABSOLUTE    waypoint_id    latitude    longitude    altitude

Parameters

waypoint_id

An integer specifying the ID to be assigned to the waypoint.

latitude

The geodetic latitude of the waypoint.

longitude

The geodetic longitude of the waypoint.

altitude

The altitude of the waypoint, specified as meters above Mean Sea Level.
