Resetting the Scenario

To reset the current scenario, click the Reset Scenario button on the main toolbar. All entities are returned to their initial states, or if an entity's state has been explicitly set by the user, the entity is returned to that state.

Note that the time of day and other environmental properties; as well as entity components, articulated parts, and special effect and weather phenomenon attributes; are not reset.
